# AI Insights Quick Reference Guide

## What Changed?

### ✅ NEW: Always Include
- **Rankings:** "2nd in NBA", "ranks 14th out of 20 teams"
- **Percentiles:** "top 8%", "bottom 25%"
- **Form Trends:** "L5: 28.4 PPG vs L10: 24.1 PPG vs L15: 22.8 PPG"
- **Context:** Every stat gets league comparison

### ❌ REMOVED: Never Include
- Generic phrases ("good scorer" → "26.8 PPG, 4th in league")
- Small differentials (no more "0.13 point difference")
- Quarter-by-quarter NBA analysis (irrelevant to fans)
- Duplicate stats across sections

## Quick Examples

### NBA Player Insights

**❌ OLD (Bad):**
- "LeBron is a strong scorer"
- "Scores more points in Q1 than Q4"
- "Good recent form"

**✅ NEW (Good):**
- "Averaging 29.3 PPG (L5) vs 24.1 (L10) - 21% increase, ranks 3rd in NBA"
- "42% from 3PT over L5 games, jumped from 34% (L10) - now 2nd among guards"
- "32.1 PPG ranks 2nd in NBA, TS% 67.2% in top 8% of league"

### NBA Team Insights

**❌ OLD (Bad):**
- "Lakers score well in Q3"
- "Team has a 0.13 PPG differential at home"
- "Strong performance in close games"

**✅ NEW (Good):**
- "115.2 ORTG ranks 3rd in NBA, but 114.7 DRTG ranks 19th - offensive powerhouse"
- "Won 8 of last 10, averaging 118.5 PPG in those wins vs 107.2 season avg"
- "12-2 at home vs 6-8 on road - 73% home win rate ranks 5th in conference"

### EPL Team Insights

**❌ OLD (Bad):**
- "Team scores goals"
- "Good attacking team"
- "Slightly better at home"

**✅ NEW (Good):**
- "2.4 goals/game ranks 3rd in EPL, with 1.6 in 2nd half vs 0.8 in 1st (2nd for 2nd half goals)"
- "Won 12 of 14 home games (86%, 3rd best) vs only 4 of 13 away (31%, 15th)"
- "14-1-0 when leading at halftime (93% win rate, 2nd in EPL) - excellent at protecting leads"

## Section Guidelines

### AI Insights (3-5 points)
**Purpose:** Key stats with context
**What to include:**
- Recent form with rankings
- Significant trends (L5 vs L10 vs L15)
- Matchup advantages
- Current streaks/momentum

### Strengths (2-3 points)
**Purpose:** Elite categories only
**What to include:**
- Top 5 rankings
- Dominant performances
- Elite efficiency metrics
**Must be:** Unique from AI Insights

### Weaknesses (2-3 points)
**Purpose:** Exploitable areas
**What to include:**
- Bottom 25% rankings
- Significant form drops (20%+ decline)
- Matchup disadvantages
**Must be:** Unique from other sections

## Sport-Specific Notes

### NBA
- ❌ No quarter-by-quarter analysis
- ✅ Focus on: L5/L10/L15 form, efficiency, home/away
- ✅ Key metrics: PPG with rankings, TS%, clutch stats

### EPL
- ✅ First/second half analysis IS relevant
- ✅ Focus on: Home/away, goals scored/conceded, halftime records
- ✅ Key metrics: Goals/game with rankings, possession %, clean sheets

### AFL
- ✅ Focus on: Goals in L5, disposals, home/away
- ✅ Key metrics: Goals/game, disposal efficiency, scoring margins

### NRL
- ✅ Focus on: Tries in L5, try assists, home/away, venue
- ✅ Key metrics: Tries/game, try assists, points for/against

### IPL
- ✅ Focus on: Strike rate, pace vs spin, venue performance
- ✅ Key metrics: SR with rankings, averages at venue, toss impact

## Significance Thresholds

Only highlight if:
- **Points:** 2+ point difference
- **Percentages:** 5%+ change
- **Rankings:** Top/bottom 25% of league
- **Form:** 15%+ change from L5 to L10/L15

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ "Team performs well" → ✅ "Won 8 of last 10, 80% win rate ranks 2nd in conference"
❌ "Slight increase in scoring" → ✅ "PPG up 18% from 24.1 to 28.4, now ranks 3rd in NBA"
❌ "Good defender" → ✅ "1.8 steals/game ranks 4th, 2.1 blocks ranks 1st among guards"
❌ Repeating same stat in insights AND strengths → ✅ Use different metrics in each section

## Quick Checklist

Before accepting any insight, verify:
- [ ] Includes specific numbers
- [ ] Includes league ranking or percentile
- [ ] Compares form windows if relevant (L5 vs L10 vs L15)
- [ ] No duplication with other sections
- [ ] Change is significant (meets thresholds)
- [ ] Relevant to fans/bettors (not random stat)
