## Key Improvements Demonstrated

### 1. Context & Rankings
- **Before:** "Good scorer"
- **After:** "32.1 PPG ranks 2nd in NBA"

### 2. Significance
- **Before:** "0.13 PPG better at home"
- **After:** Only mentions differentials of 2+ points or 5%+

### 3. Form Trends
- **Before:** "Recent good form"
- **After:** "29.3 PPG (L5) vs 24.1 (L10) vs 22.8 (L15) - 21% increase"

### 4. Relevance
- **Before:** "Scores more in Q1 than Q4"
- **After:** "7-3 in games decided by 5 or less, 2nd best clutch record"

### 5. Specificity
- **Before:** "Strong defensive team"
- **After:** "106.2 PPG allowed, 3rd best defense in NBA"

### 6. No Duplication
- **Before:** Same stat in insights and strengths
- **After:** Each section has completely unique metrics

## Summary

Every insight now includes:
- ✅ Specific numbers from data
- ✅ League rankings or percentiles
- ✅ Form comparisons (L5 vs L10 vs L15)
- ✅ Relevant context for fans/bettors
- ✅ No small/insignificant differentials
- ✅ No irrelevant quarter-by-quarter analysis
- ✅ No duplication between sections
- ✅ No generic statements
